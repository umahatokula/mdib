Pre-marriage School
Singles

Marriage preparation is not only important but absolutely necessary to get you started your marriage covenant adventure on a good foundation.

Research shows that couples who receive premarital Counselling have a 30% lower chance at divorce than couples who do not. (Scott Stanley, The Marriage Project.)

That’s why Marriage Prep School is a great place to get you started.

Marriage Prep School is a 4-week Premarital Counselling Program.

You could choose to take the courses online or in person, depending on your location.

What you’ll learn in 4 weeks

  • Your identity
  • How it affects your choice of a life partner
  • The role of your faith and spirituality in choosing a life partner
  • How to know whether or not you’re ready for marriage
  • How to know "The One"
  • How to become Mr/Mrs Right
  • Communication skills
  • Conflict management /resolution
  • How to become financially intelligent
  • A ton of questions to ask a potential spouse
  • And more

A certificate of participation will be presented to every active participant at the end of the program.
