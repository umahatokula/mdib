About Me

Hi, I'm Doofan Hiikyaa

I am beloved of the Father in Christ Jesus; the happy, virtuous wife of a faithful man, Orgem and we are blessed with two beautiful daughters Ken-ana and Verse.

I am a marriage counsellor/coach, writer and first time author, host of the Flawless Marriage Conference and the founder of *MARRIAGE DIPPED IN BLOOD* a ministry focused on love, marriage and family relationships from a covenant perspective.

My all-consuming obsession is to restore families and lead many to understand and enjoy a lifelong happy and fruitful marriage in the bond of covenant.

I am confident that every troubled marriage can be restored with fresh passion and every thriving marriage can be better, if we remain conscious of marriage as a blood covenant and allow God’s Word—The Bible, be our manual of operation.

Orgem and I are often referred to as "Apostles of Love" and currently serve as the Resident Pastors of Christ Family Centre, Abidjan, Côte d'Ivoire.
